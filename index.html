Methodology

This directory serves as a high-level reference for developers and technical leads assessing infrastructure stability. The comparison prioritizes Time-to-First-Byte (TTFB) stability, deployment automation, and resource isolation. Legacy shared hosting environments are excluded from enterprise-grade consideration due to inherent resource contention and lack of native containerization.
